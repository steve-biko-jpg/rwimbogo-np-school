Our Programs

Our Academic Programs

We follow the National Curriculum, offering a comprehensive education from nursery to primary level. Each program is designed to provide a strong foundation for future learning and development.

Nursery School

Our nursery program provides a fun and engaging environment for our youngest learners. We focus on early literacy, numeracy, and social skills through play-based activities.

Classes: Baby Class, Middle Class, Top Class

Duration: 3 years

Download Timetable

Lower Primary

The lower primary section builds a solid academic foundation. We concentrate on core subjects and foster critical thinking and problem-solving skills.

Classes: P1, P2, P3

Duration: 3 years

Download Timetable

Upper Primary

Our upper primary program prepares students for the next stage of their academic journey. The curriculum becomes more in-depth, encouraging independent learning and preparation for national examinations.

Classes: P4, P5, P6

Duration: 3 years

Extracurricular Activities

Education extends beyond the classroom. We offer a variety of extracurricular activities to help students explore their talents, build teamwork, and develop a well-rounded character.

Debate

Develops public speaking and critical thinking skills.

Football

Promotes teamwork, fitness, and sportsmanship.

Basketball

Enhances coordination, agility, and competitive spirit.

Running

Builds endurance and a healthy, active lifestyle.

Music

Fosters creativity and discipline through musical expression.
