Admissions

General Admission Criteria

We welcome all prospective students who meet the following age requirements:

  • Nursery One: Must be 3 years old and above.
  • Primary One: Must be 6 years old and above.

Required Documents

To complete the admission process, please prepare the following documents:

  • Birth Certificate
  • Academic report from previous school (for continuing learners)
  • Student Identification
  • Parental or Guardian Contact Information

Ready for Your Child's Future?

We are ready to guide your child towards a future of excellence and purpose.
