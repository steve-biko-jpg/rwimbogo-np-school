About Our School

Our Story

Rwimbogo Nursery and Primary School is a Christian school located in Kanombe, near the Airport in Kigali, Rwanda. We have been providing quality, holistic education since 2001. With over 23 years of experience, we are dedicated to nurturing and empowering a smart generation of learners.

Our Guiding Principles

Our Mission

To empower through holistic quality education for a smart generation.

Our Vision

Enabling learners to acquire skills, knowledge, and values that will allow them to achieve their fullest potential.

Our Values

Our core values are Excellence, Charity, and Solidarity, which we instill in every student to prepare them for a life of purpose and service.
